# **Niko (निको)**

Niko is an AI-powered unified health platform that securely connects patient records across hospitals while preventing medical fraud through cryptographic verification.

# **1. Executive Summary**

**Niko (निको)** is a first-of-its-kind Health Operating System designed for nations where data fragmentation and document fraud are systemic barriers to care. It transforms medical records from static, untrusted files into Verified Clinical Assets. By fusing Hierarchical PKI (Public Key Infrastructure) for tamper-proof reports with Agentic AI (RAG) for real-time safety, Niko empowers doctors to save lives and governments to stop pandemics before they start.

# **2. The "Trilemma" (The Problem)**

Existing systems fail because they solve only one part of the problem. Niko solves the three-way crisis:
• **The Data Silo:** Patient history is trapped in paper or local servers. In an emergency, a Blind Doctor is a dangerous doctor.
• **The Truth Gap:** Digital reports are currently just images that anyone can Photoshop. This costs the insurance industry millions and compromises border health security.
• **The Response Lag:** Governments rely on manual reporting. By the time an outbreak is reported, the virus has already spread.

# **3. The Niko Solution: Four Pillars of Innovation**

## Pillar 1: Niko Verify (The Cryptographic Trust Layer)

We eliminate fraud at the source.

- **The Tech:** Using a SHA-256 **Hashing** algorithm, every report is digitally signed in a three-step hierarchy: **Hospital → Medical Director → Ministry of Health.**
- **The Result:** A QR code that proves authenticity offline. If a single character on the report is altered, the cryptographic hash fails. This is The Gold Standard for legal medical documents.

## Pillar 2: Niko Flow & Guide (Smart Check-in & Routing)

We eliminate the physical queue bottleneck found in major hospitals.

- **Instant Token:** Patients scan a hospital QR code to register instantly. This creates a verified track record in the Niko grid.
- **Dynamic Routing:** Instead of waiting in a static line, Niko checks real-time doctor availability. It tells the patient exactly which room to go to, This balances hospital load and prevents hallway crowding.
- **Patient Freedom:** Patients can wait in fresh air or a cafe; the app tracks their turn and alerts them when the doctor is ready.

## Pillar 3: Niko Lens & Guard (AI Clinical Intelligence)

Doctors don’t have time for 50-page PDFs, and human error is a constant risk.

- **The Lens:** Our Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) engine scans the digital patient history and generates a 10-Second Life-Saving Brief (Allergies, Chronic Conditions, Last Surgery).
- **The Guard:** An AI Agent monitors current prescriptions against history. If a doctor prescribes a drug that conflicts with a condition from 2 years ago, Niko triggers a Red Alert immediately.

## Pillar 4: Niko Pulse (National Epidemic Radar)

- **The Tech:** Anonymized syndromic data (fever, cough, rashes) from the Niko Flow check-ins and Niko Verify reports is piped into a geospatial heatmap.
- **The Impact:** If a cluster of cases appears in a specific Municipality, Niko Pulse sends an automated alert to MoHP officials. Niko is the Early Warning System that prevents the next lockdown.

# **4. Future Enhancement (Scalability)**

• **Niko Pay:** Integrated insurance claim processing verified by the crypto-signature.
